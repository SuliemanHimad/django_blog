Author Bio Sulaiman Hamad (Arabic: سليمان حمد; born 19 May 1994, in Jeddah) is a Saudi judoka. He competed at the 2016 Summer Olympics in the men's 66 kg, in which he was eliminated in the second round by Davaadorjiin Tömörkhüleg. He was the flag bearer for Saudi Arabia at the Parade of Nations. World Champ.
